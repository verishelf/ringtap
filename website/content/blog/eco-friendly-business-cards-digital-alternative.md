Paper business cards use trees, water, and energy to produce—and many end up in the trash. Digital business cards are a sustainable alternative.

## The problem with paper

- **Resource use** — Paper production uses water, energy, and trees.
- **Waste** — Cards are often discarded or lost.
- **Updates** — Job changes or new contact info make old cards obsolete.
- **Reordering** — Constant reprints add more waste.

## Why digital is greener

- **No physical waste** — No paper, ink, or plastic.
- **Always current** — Update your info in the app; no reprints.
- **Reusable** — One link, QR, or NFC product works indefinitely.
- **Less transport** — No shipping of boxes of cards.

## NFC rings and cards

NFC rings and cards are durable and reusable. One ring can share your profile for years. When you change jobs or contact info, update the app—the ring stays the same, the link stays the same, and everyone sees the latest.

## Small change, big impact

If you hand out 500 cards a year, switching to digital saves paper, ink, and shipping. Multiply that across teams and industries, and the impact adds up.
