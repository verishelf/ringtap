A **digital business card** is a contact card you share online or in person—via a link, QR code, or NFC tap—instead of a physical paper card. When someone opens your link or scans your QR, they see your name, photo, contact info, social links, and any other details you choose to share.

## What makes it different from a paper card?

- **Always up to date** — Change your info in the app anytime; your link stays the same.
- **No app required** — Recipients open your profile in their browser. They can save your contact or tap your links without installing anything.
- **Instant sharing** — Tap an NFC ring or card, or scan a QR code, and your profile opens in seconds.
- **Trackable** — See how many people view your profile and which links they click.

## How do you share it?

1. **Link** — Your personal URL (e.g. `ringtap.me/yourname`) that you share anywhere.
2. **QR code** — Built into the RingTap app; others scan it with their camera.
3. **NFC** — Program an NFC ring or card with your link; tap it to your phone to share.

## Who should use one?

- **Professionals** — Sales, consultants, freelancers, and anyone who networks often.
- **Event attendees** — Conferences, trade shows, meetups.
- **Students and job seekers** — Easy way to share a portfolio and contact info.
- **Small businesses** — One link for the whole team or a branded presence.
