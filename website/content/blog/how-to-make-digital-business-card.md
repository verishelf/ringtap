You can create a digital business card in about five minutes. Here’s the simplest path.

## Step 1: Choose an app

Pick an app that gives you a personal link (e.g. `ringtap.me/yourname`), a QR code, and doesn’t require recipients to install anything. RingTap, Popl, Blinq, and HiHello all work.

## Step 2: Sign up and set your username

Create an account and choose a username. This becomes your link: `ringtap.me/username`. Keep it short and easy to say.

## Step 3: Add your info

Fill in:

- Name and photo
- Title or role
- Email and phone
- Bio (1–2 sentences)
- Links (LinkedIn, portfolio, website, etc.)

## Step 4: Share it

- **Link** — Share `ringtap.me/yourname` in email, text, or social.
- **QR code** — Open the QR in the app; others scan it with their camera.
- **NFC** — If you have an NFC ring or card, use the app to write your link to it.

## Step 5: Update anytime

Change your info in the app whenever you need to. Your link stays the same; everyone sees the latest version.

That’s it. RingTap is free to start—download, set your username, add your details, and share.
