RingTap, Popl, and Blinq are popular digital business card apps. Here’s how they compare.

## Quick comparison

| Feature | RingTap | Popl | Blinq |
|---------|---------|------|-------|
| Free tier | Yes | Limited | Limited |
| Personal link | ringtap.me/you | popl.co/you | blinq.me/you |
| QR code | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| NFC ring/card | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| No app for recipients | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Custom themes | Pro | Pro | Pro |
| Analytics | Pro | Pro | Pro |
| Custom QR with logo | Pro | Pro | Pro |

## RingTap

- **Free** — Link, QR, basic profile. Recipients never need the app.
- **Pro** — Unlimited links, themes, analytics, NFC, custom QR, video intro.
- **NFC** — Sells rings and cards; can program compatible third-party NFC products.

## Popl

- **Free** — Limited features; upgrade for full link and NFC.
- **Pro** — Full features, branded Popl products.
- **Strong in** — Popl-branded hardware and team plans.

## Blinq

- **Free** — Basic card; upgrade for more.
- **Pro** — Teams, CRM integration, analytics.
- **Strong in** — Team and enterprise use cases.

## Which to choose

- **Solo, want free + NFC** — RingTap’s free tier includes QR and link; Pro adds NFC and analytics.
- **Team or CRM** — Blinq may fit better.
- **Popl hardware** — If you prefer Popl-branded devices, Popl is a good option.

All three work without requiring recipients to install an app. Try the free tiers and see which flow you prefer.
