A QR code business card is a QR code that points to your digital profile. When someone scans it with their phone camera, your profile opens in their browser.

## How it works

1. **Create your profile** — Set up your digital business card in an app like RingTap.
2. **Get your QR code** — The app generates a QR code linked to your profile URL.
3. **Share it** — Show the QR on your phone, print it on paper, or add it to a card or sticker.
4. **They scan** — They open their camera, point at the QR, and tap the notification. Your profile opens.

## No app for recipients

They don’t need to install anything. iPhone and Android cameras can read QR codes natively. They just scan and tap.

## Where to use it

- **Phone screen** — Show your QR when meeting someone.
- **Email signature** — Embed a QR image linking to your profile.
- **Printed materials** — Business cards, flyers, brochures.
- **Signage** — Trade show booths, office lobbies.
- **Social media** — Post your QR or link in your bio.
