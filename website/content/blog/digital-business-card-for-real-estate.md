Real estate agents hand out a lot of contact info—at open houses, showings, and networking events. A digital business card keeps it all in one link.

## What agents put on their card

- **Contact** — Phone, email, preferred way to reach
- **Listings** — Link to your MLS, Zillow, or website
- **Calendar** — Booking link for showings or consultations
- **Social** — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn
- **Testimonials** — Link to reviews or a testimonial page

## How to share

- **Open houses** — QR code on a sign or flyer; visitors scan to save your contact and see listings.
- **Showings** — Tap an NFC ring or card to their phone for instant contact save.
- **Networking** — Share your link at events or in follow-up emails.
- **Email signature** — Add `ringtap.me/yourname` so every email includes your full profile.

## Benefits

- No running out of paper cards
- Listings and links always up to date
- See who viewed your profile (with Pro)
- One link for buyers, sellers, and referrals
