## What to look for

- **No app for recipients** — Your profile should open in the browser. RingTap and most modern apps do this.
- **NFC support** — If you want a tap-to-share ring or card, check that the app can write to NFC.
- **Custom link** — A short URL like `ringtap.me/you` is easy to remember and share.
- **Analytics** — See profile views and link clicks. Pro features often include this.
- **Themes** — Accent colors, fonts, and layout options make your card stand out.
