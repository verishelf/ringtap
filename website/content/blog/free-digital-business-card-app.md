You can get a working digital business card for free—no credit card required. Here’s what most free tiers include and what you might want to upgrade for.

## What’s usually free

- **Personal link** — A URL like `ringtap.me/yourname` you can share anywhere
- **QR code** — Built-in QR in the app; others scan it to open your profile
- **Basic profile** — Name, photo, title, bio, email, phone, social links
- **Limited links** — Often 3–5 links on the free plan
- **No app for recipients** — They open your profile in their browser

## What’s typically paid (Pro)

- **Unlimited links** — Add as many links as you need
- **Custom themes** — Accent color, fonts, button shape
- **Analytics** — Profile views, link clicks, NFC taps
- **NFC support** — Write your link to an NFC ring or card
- **Custom QR** — Your logo in the center, theme colors
- **Video intro** — Short video on your profile
- **Contact sync** — Save contacts to your phone
