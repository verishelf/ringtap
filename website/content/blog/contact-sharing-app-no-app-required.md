A good contact sharing app should let you share your info quickly—and let the other person view and save it without installing an app.

## Why “no app for recipients” matters

- **Friction** — Asking someone to download an app to get your contact is a barrier.
- **Speed** — They can open your link in their browser and save your contact in seconds.
- **Universal** — Works on any phone with a browser.

## How it works

1. **You** — Use the app to share your profile via link, QR code, or NFC tap.
2. **They** — Open the link in their browser. No download.
3. **Save** — They tap “Save contact” or “Add to contacts” and your info goes to their phone.

## What to look for

- **Browser-based profile** — Your link opens a web page, not an app store.
- **Save contact** — One tap to add you to their contacts.
- **Works on iPhone and Android** — Recipients on any device can view and save.
