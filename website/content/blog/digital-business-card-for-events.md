Events are where digital business cards shine. No fumbling for paper, no running out of cards, and you can follow up with everyone who viewed your profile.

## Why digital works at events

- **Speed** — Tap or scan in seconds. No typing emails or swapping cards.
- **Always current** — Update your info before the event; everyone sees the latest.
- **Trackable** — See who viewed your profile and when.
- **No waste** — No printing, no leftover cards.

## Best ways to share at events

1. **NFC ring** — Tap your ring to their phone. Fast and memorable. Great for high-volume networking.
2. **QR code** — Show your QR on your phone or a small card. They scan with their camera.
3. **Link** — Share `ringtap.me/you` verbally or via text. Works when you’re not in the same room.

## Tips for event networking

- Set your username before the event so your link is short and easy to say.
- Add your LinkedIn, portfolio, or calendar link so they can book a call.
- Use Pro analytics to see who viewed your profile and follow up.
- At trade shows, consider an NFC card or QR sticker on your booth.
