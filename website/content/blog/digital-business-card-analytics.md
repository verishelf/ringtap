With a digital business card, you can see how many people viewed your profile and which links they clicked. That data helps you follow up and improve your card.

## What to track

- **Profile views** — How many people opened your profile.
- **Link clicks** — Which links (LinkedIn, portfolio, etc.) got the most taps.
- **NFC taps** — How many people tapped your ring or card (Pro feature).
- **QR scans** — How many people scanned your QR code (Pro feature).
- **Time and source** — When and where views happened (if the app supports it).

## Why it matters

- **Follow-up** — If someone viewed your profile after a meeting, you have a reason to reach out.
- **Optimization** — If one link gets far more clicks, highlight it or put it first.
- **ROI** — See if events or campaigns drive profile views.
- **Validation** — Confirm your NFC or QR sharing is working.

## How to use the data

1. **After events** — Check views and link clicks; follow up with high-intent viewers.
2. **A/B test** — Change link order or wording and see what gets more clicks.
3. **Content** — Add or remove links based on what people actually use.
